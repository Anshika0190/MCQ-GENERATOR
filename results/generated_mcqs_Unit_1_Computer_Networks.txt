## MCQ 1
Question: What is the primary function of a network?
A) To store files securely
B) To share resources, exchange files, and enable communication
C) To provide entertainment
D) To perform complex calculations

Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: How many layers are in the OSI reference model?
A) 4
B) 5
C) 6
D) 7

Correct Answer: D

## MCQ 3
Question: Which layer of the TCP/IP model is responsible for end-to-end communication and error-free delivery of data?
A) Application Layer
B) Internet Layer
C) Transport Layer
D) Network Access Layer

Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 4
Question: What is the function of a repeater in a network?
A) To amplify the signal
B) To regenerate the signal at its original strength
C) To filter content by reading MAC addresses
D) To connect multiple devices in a network

Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 5
Question: Which network topology connects all devices to a central hub or switch?
A) Bus Topology
B) Ring Topology
C) Star Topology
D) Mesh Topology

Question: What is attenuation in the context of transmission media?
