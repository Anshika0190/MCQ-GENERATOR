## MCQ 1
Question: Where did the Shakespear tree stand?
A) In a dense forest
B) On a mountaintop
C) In the town square of Eldermere
D) By the riverbank
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 2
Question: What was unusual about the Shakespear tree's fruit?
A) They were shaped like apples.
B) They were poisonous.
C) They shimmered with a golden hue.
D) They grew only in winter.
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question: What did the villagers believe about the Shakespear tree?
A) It brought bad luck.
B) It held magical properties.
C) It was cursed.
D) It attracted dangerous animals.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 4
Question: What was Emma's dream?
A) To become a painter
B) To become a writer
C) To travel the world
D) To become a scientist
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 5
Question: What did Emma see in her visions after eating the Shakespear?
A) Herself failing at everything she tried
B) Herself standing on a stage receiving applause and wandering through enchanted forests

## MCQ 6

## MCQ 7
